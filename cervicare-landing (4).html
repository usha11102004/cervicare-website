The Crisis

Women Are Dying — Because Nobody Caught It In Time

Every year, 3,50,000 women lose their lives to cervical cancer globally. In rural India, clinics are hours away, tests are expensive, and most women have never had a single cervical screening in their lives.

Late diagnosis leads to higher mortality, financial devastation for families, and an overwhelming burden on the healthcare system — for a cancer that is almost entirely preventable.

🏥Clinics are hours away from rural areas
💸Screening tests cost more than a week's income
😶Zero awareness about symptoms and risk factors
⏰Diagnosed too late — when it's Stage 3 or 4

Our Solution

How Cervicare Works

Any local clinic can use Cervicare to screen women for cervical cancer — instantly, accurately, and affordably — with no specialist required.

1 📸
Upload Image
Clinic staff uploads a cervical colposcopy image directly into our secure platform — from any device
2 🤖
AI Analyses
Our deep learning CNN model processes and analyses the image with high-accuracy pattern recognition
3 📊
Get Results
Instant risk classification — Low, Medium, or High — delivered in seconds, not days
4 👩‍⚕️
Doctor Acts
The doctor receives a clear report and advises the patient on the spot — no lab wait needed
